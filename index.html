About Me

I am a very curious and creative individual. I have 2 degrees in Psychology, Criminology and law and another degree in International Business and Commerce. I have worked in various fields from being a Public Relation Manager, Executive Secretary to working in an advertising agency. I have lived and worked in various countries.I have an eclectic taste in everything, from books, art, music and knowledge.

I understand the human psych far too well and I am now learning the "psych", if you will, of coding and programming. I am focused, diligent and persistent. I have just about begun my journey with technology. My current knowledge of computers is in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, I will soon begin my journey in the world of back-end development. I intend to be keep updating myself with programming so I may enhance my creativity with all that is available to me. My imagination is limitless and ongoing and my ability to understand people and behaviour, gives me an upper hand in understanding not only what you require, but also the end user.
